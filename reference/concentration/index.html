Concentration functions¶

The package makes available functions relating to sediment concentration.

The functions are defined in proffilo.concentration.

convert_mass_to_volumetric(mass_conc[, density])

Convert mass concentration to volumetric concentration.

convert_volumetric_to_mass(vol_conc[, density])

Convert volumetric concentration to mass concentration.
