proffilo.station.Station¶

class proffilo.station.Station(ID, bed_distribution=None, flow_depth=None, ustar=None, slope=None, nearbed_distribution=None, nearbed_concentration=None)¶

Survey station entity.

Survey station class. Provides organization to other objects that are related to the survey station. Many object can be initialized from Station methods, or by assignment (see examples below). Additionally provides a number of helpful plotting and visualization functions.

ID¶

Identification string for the survey station, should be a unique identifier.
